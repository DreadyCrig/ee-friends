Installation

  1. Within the /system/expressionengine/third_party/ directory, copy the friends folder into your sites ./system/expressionengine/third_party/ directory.
  2. Within the /themes/third_party/ directory, copy the friends folder into your sites ./themes/third_party/ directory.
  3. Go to the Modules area in the ExpressionEngine control panel, find Friends in the list, and click Install.
  4. Setup and configuring:
    • If you wish to install the demo templates, click on the Demo Templates tab inside the Control Panel area.

Updating

  1. Within your sites ./system/expressionengine/third_party/ directory, delete the friends folder and upload the new one from the /system/expressionengine/third_party/ directory in your download package.
  2. Within your sites ./themes/third_party/ directory, delete the friends folder and upload the new one from the /themes/third_party/ directory in your download package.
  3. Go to the Modules area in the ExpressionEngine control panel and click the Run Module Updates button.
